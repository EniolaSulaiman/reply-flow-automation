FAQ

Questions, Answered.

Everything you need to know before getting started with AI customer support automation for e-commerce.

Is ReplyFlow compatible with Shopify?

Yes. ReplyFlow is a certified Shopify partner with a 1-click install from the Shopify App Store. It syncs your orders, customers, products, and fulfillment data automatically — no developer required. WooCommerce is also fully supported.

Does ReplyFlow replace human agents?

No — and it's not designed to. ReplyFlow handles the 80% of repetitive, rule-based tickets automatically, freeing your human agents to focus on complex or high-value interactions. It also escalates sensitive cases to humans proactively.

How secure is my customer data?

ReplyFlow is SOC 2 Type II compliant and GDPR-ready. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256. We never sell or share customer data, and you retain full ownership of all store and customer information at all times.

Can I customize the AI's responses?

Absolutely. During onboarding, you provide your return policy, FAQs, tone guidelines, and any custom rules. ReplyFlow learns your brand voice and generates responses that match your style — not generic chatbot language. You can refine responses anytime.

What happens if the AI makes a mistake?

Any response below a confidence threshold is automatically flagged and routed to a human agent rather than sent. You can review flagged responses, correct the AI, and it learns from each correction. Our average accuracy rate across active stores is 97.4%.

How long does setup take?

Most stores are live within 24 hours. The store connection takes under 5 minutes. Training the AI on your policies takes another 30–60 minutes. After that, ReplyFlow is handling tickets automatically — typically hitting the 80% automation rate within the first 7 days.
